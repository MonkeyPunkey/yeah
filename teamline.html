[Italian]

During the Ironhack bootcamp, I worked on several projects, one of them was a web app. I had to create a project management tool (in a MPV version) and I had to interview people from tech industry.

Investigate

The first thing that I did was to know better the competitors. There are a lot of project management tools,like Trello, Jira, Asana and so on. After my researchs I interviewed three people by phone which work in the Deloitte company in Berlin. My interviews were very interesting, they usually use Trello or Jira, but not everyone in Deloitte and in their teams, so it's not always easy to communicate and organize the work. From these interviews I was able to have my persona.

Conclusions

Doing this web app was very interesting, I had the opportunity to talk with lovely people and know a really big company. This is a minimum viable product, so in the next months there will be several improvements. I focused on three things until now: having a calendar, boards and one chat to communicate with your team.
